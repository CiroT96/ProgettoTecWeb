What is it?

It is a product that offers itself to the needs of those who want to approach programming for the first time and/or those who want to improve their skills and competences in the subject.

Who does it address?

The product is aimed primarily at those who have seen the opportunities that exist in this field and realized that this is the time to act. It is a training path suitable for all those who want to try their hand in the world of programming, to use the application does not require any special requirements or skills if not a passion for the subject and a constant commitment to finish the proposed levels.

What is it for?

The product allows users to approach various elements of basic programming. With the possibility of gaining experience in programming and getting to have skills in this field.
